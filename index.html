Thanks for using DozeOff Pro! Here's how to use the app:

How to Use

  1. Play music or audio of your choice
  2. Open the DozeOff Pro app
  3. Set your desired time
  4. Press Start
  5. Either leave your phone open, or press the lock button
  6. Your audio will pause when the timer ends
